About Me

I am an Account Manager at Flores & Associates, a third-party voluntary benefits and COBRA administrator located in Southend Charlotte. I am originally from Beaufort, NC and moved to Charlotte for college. I attended Queens University of Charlotte and graduated with a Bachelors degree in English, with a concentration in secondary education. I taught 8th grade English for five years, before I transitioned into my current role. I live in the Steele Creek neighborhood of Charlotte with my husband and our two dogs: Duke the Dachshund and Hank the Beagle mix. My goal is to learn coding and web development to not only continuing challenging myself, but to further my career internally by enhancing the overall customer service experience with our technology.
